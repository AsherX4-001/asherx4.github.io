The Rogue

A Hardcore Excel RPG by AsherX4_001

About the Game

The Rogue is a survival RPG built entirely inside Microsoft Excel using VBA — no external art, no fancy engines. Just pure code, logic, and patience. Survive, upgrade, and face brutal challenges that even the creator struggles to beat.

Download The Rogue (ZIP)

Note: Requires an activated version of Microsoft Excel with macros enabled. Do not purchase or play if you cannot enable macros — the game depends on it.

Creator’s Message

I built this game for those who love simplicity, logic, and old-school challenge. It’s meant to be tough, frustrating, and rewarding. Expect bugs, expect losses, but also — expect to smile when you finally win.
